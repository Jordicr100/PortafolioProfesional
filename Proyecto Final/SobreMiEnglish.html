About Me

My name is Jordi Garro Solergibert, I'm 22 years old, I'm from Costa Rica, and I work as a full-stack software developer.

I consider myself a passionate person when it comes to programming, both in front-end and back-end development, and I love working on it. My specialty is in web application development, and I also have experience in desktop application and video game development.

I am someone who enjoys learning new things, constantly seeking new information. My curiosity and passion for learning have led me to explore various areas of technology and development. With a strong foundation in full-stack development, I have worked on projects ranging from web applications to complex database systems.

I always strive to improve my skills, adopting new technologies and methodologies that allow optimizing processes and creating more efficient solutions. I enjoy facing new challenges and collaborating with others to achieve common goals.

I believe teamwork, open communication, and continuous improvement are essential for success. My focus is always on creating solutions that are not only functional but also provide an exceptional user experience.
